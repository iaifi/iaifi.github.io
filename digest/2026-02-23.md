### Networking Lunch with Colloquium Speaker this Friday, February 27, 2026

IAIFI students and postdocs are invited to an informal, catered networking lunch with IAIFI’s colloquium speaker Andrew Wilson (NYU). Lunch and refreshments will be provided. The event will be at 12:30pm on Friday, February 27, at the Penthouse (26-648). Andrew’s talk will be at 2:00pm in the Kolker Room (MIT 26-414).

### Thematic Discussion Recap: LLMS and VLMs for Science
Thank you to everyone who attended our thematic discussion session this past Friday on *LLMs and VLMs for Science*, and thank you especially to the discussion's host Nikhil Mukund (MIT/LIGO), and speakers Thomas Harvey (IAIFI Fellow) and Mike Williams (IAIFI Interim Director). Check out the recording and slides at the links below.

Following up on the discussion, if you want to contribute to an IAIFI blog post regarding LLMs and VLMs for Science, reach out to [iaifi@mit.edu](mailto:iaifi@mit.edu).
