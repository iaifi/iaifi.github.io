## News from IAIFI Management

### Career Support Resources for Internships, International Students, and More
The Talent Forum has compiled [lists of career resources](https://iaifi.org/career-support.html#university-resources) at MIT, Harvard, Tufts, and Northeastern. These include career advice resources, alumni hubs, internship resources, and more. If you have any additional resources you would like to share, please let us know.

[Career Resources](https://iaifi.org/career-support.html#university-resources){:.button.button--outline-primary.button--pill.button--sm} [Find other resources in the IAIFI README doc](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1RjddJR6ER4mlXq438C5RkAIv-ZisUPwibTm52-2o_Yw/edit?usp=sharing){:.button.button--outline-primary.button--pill.button--sm} 

### Participation Survey: Response required from Junior Investigators by Feb. 1
In [this form](https://app.smartsheet.com/b/form/019b08a8526a77f3bb65013da5a61e94), you can reflect on your level of participation in Fall 2025, indicate your expected participation in Spring 2026, and provide input on some key events (such as a potential “IAIFI Industry Day”), among other things. The form should take less than 15 minutes to complete.

IAIFI Junior Investigators are required to complete the form, but all IAIFI members are welcome to fill it out & provide input. Filling out this form helps our organizing teams ensure our activities and events are as beneficial as possible to the IAIFI community.

[Complete the Survey](https://app.smartsheet.com/b/form/019b08a8526a77f3bb65013da5a61e94){:.button.button--outline-primary.button--pill.button--sm}
