## News from IAIFI Management

### IAIFI Summer School Applications open until February 9
Apply to the 2026 IAIFI Summer School by February 9, 2026! The summer school will feature lectures, hands-on tutorials, lightning talks, a hackathon, and networking events. All IAIFI Junior Investigators are guaranteed acceptance in person at least once.

[Learn more and apply](https://iaifi.org/phd-summer-school.html){:.button.button--outline-primary.button--pill.button--sm} 

### Participation Survey: Response required from Junior Investigators by Feb. 1
In [this form](https://app.smartsheet.com/b/form/019b08a8526a77f3bb65013da5a61e94), you can reflect on your level of participation in Fall 2025, indicate your expected participation in Spring 2026, and provide input on some key events (such as a potential “IAIFI Industry Day”), among other things. The form should take less than 15 minutes to complete.

IAIFI Junior Investigators are required to complete the form, but all IAIFI members are welcome to fill it out & provide input. Completing the form helps our organizing teams ensure our activities and events are as beneficial as possible to the IAIFI community.

[Fill out the survey](https://app.smartsheet.com/b/form/019b08a8526a77f3bb65013da5a61e94){:.button.button--outline-primary.button--pill.button--sm}
