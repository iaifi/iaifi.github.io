### Last Lunch Discussion of Spring 2026 this Thursday: LLMs and the Future of Research Training

Our final IAIFI Lunch Discussion of Spring 2026 is this Thursday, May 14 at 1:00pm. Hosted by Sean Benevedes (MIT) and Victoria Zhang (MIT), this will be a follow-up to February's discussion, "How do LLMs impact work for students & postdocs?" For this session, the topic will be "LLMs and the Future of Research Training." We hope you can join the discussion, either at the Penthouse or on Zoom.
