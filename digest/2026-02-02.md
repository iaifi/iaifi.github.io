### IAIFI Journal Club, now Lunch Discussions for Spring 2026
Sign-ups are open for IAIFI Lunch Discussions for Spring 2026! These events will be **Thursdays at 1:00pm**, at the IAIFI Penthouse and on Zoom, with lunch provided. Events will be advertised as they are confirmed.

Previously called the "IAIFI Journal Club," the Lunch Discussions will be a great opportunity to share your own research, discuss larger-scale themes at the intersection of AI+Physics, and/or spark new ideas and collaborations. The discussions will also be followed by the weekly **IAIFI Coffee Hour on Thursdays at 2:00 pm**, which all are welcome to attend regardless of attendance at the Lunch Discussions. 

Fill out the form below to volunteer to lead a discussion, or to suggest a discussion topic for a future event (such as a particular research paper of interest, a trend in ethical research considerations, etc.)! We look forward to your input.

[Lunch Discussion Form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSf6_sXJqBoVirNvf_H7lo6OkyxEHNwjBBuOc-XBkWWJcXN36g/viewform){:.button.button--outline-primary.button--pill.button--sm}
