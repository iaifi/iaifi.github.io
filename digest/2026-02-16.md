### First Thematic Discussion Session of Spring 2026 on Friday, February 20
Our first Thematic Discussion Session of the semester will be this coming Friday, February 20, 2:00pm at the Kolker Room (MIT 26-414) and on Zoom. The discussion will be on *LLMs and VLMs for Science*, and it will be led by Nikhil Mukund (MIT), with lightning talks by Thomas Harvey (IAIFI Fellow) and Mike Williams (IAIFI Interim Director). Check out more information in the *Events This Week* section. Refreshments will be provided.
