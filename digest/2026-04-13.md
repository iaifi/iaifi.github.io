### Thematic Discussion on Reinforcement Learning on Friday, April 17
Join us for our Thematic Discussion on Reinforcement Learning this coming Friday, April 17, at 2:00pm in the Kolker Room (MIT 26-414) and on Zoom. The event will begin with three lightning talks (10 minutes each, plus short Q&A) regarding different aspects of Reinforcement Learning, followed by a guided discussion. Speakers will include Jacob Adamczyk (UMass Boston), ‘Statistical Mechanics and Reinforcement Learning’; Christina Reissel (IAIFI Fellow), ‘Reinforcement Learning for Experimental Control: Taming the Tremors at LIGO’; and Fabian Ruehle (Northeastern), ‘Gamifying Mathematics.’ Check out the details for each lightning talk here: https://tinyurl.com/yaa2ae43

### Save the Date: Industry and Academia Career Advice Round Table on Thursday, May 7
Our May 7 IAIFI Lunch Discussion will be a special-edition *Industry and Academia Career Advice Round Table*. Panelists will include several IAIFI members with a wide range of experiences, including Aizhan Akhmetzhanova (Harvard), Peter Lu (Tufts), Siddharth Mishra-Sharma (Boston University), and Jose Munoz (MIT). Further details to come; lunch and refreshments will be provided. The event will be 1:00-2:00pm at the IAIFI Penthouse and on Zoom.
