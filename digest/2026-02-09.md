### Kicking off Spring 2026 IAIFI Events
**IAIFI Lunch Discussion (formerly Journal Club) this Thursday:** Our IAIFI Lunch Discussion organizers, Sean Benevedes (MIT) and Victoria Zhang (MIT), will host our first event this coming Thursday, February 12, at 1:00pm at the Penthouse and on Zoom! The discussion is titled: *How do LLMs impact work for students & postdocs?* Check out the full abstract in the "Events This Week" section below. Lunch and refreshments will be provided, and feel free to invite people from outside of IAIFI you think may be interested!

*We are still looking for volunteers to lead lunch discussions; this can be a presentation on your own work or simply leading a discussion on a topic of interest. [Sign up here](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSf6_sXJqBoVirNvf_H7lo6OkyxEHNwjBBuOc-XBkWWJcXN36g/viewform)!*

**IAIFI Colloquium with Roger Melko this Friday:** Join us on Friday, February 13, for the first public colloquium of the semester: *Language Models for Quantum Simulation*, with visiting speaker [Roger Melko](https://rgmelko.github.io/) (University of Waterloo). Roger's talk will be at 2:00pm ET, in the MIT Kolker Room and on Zoom. Check out the talk abstract in the "Events This Week" section below. 

*Earlier in the day, IAIFI students and postdocs are invited to an informal, catered networking lunch with Roger. The lunch will be at 12:30pm at the IAIFI Penthouse.*
