### First IAIFI Colloquium this Friday
Join us on Friday, February 13, for the first public colloquium of the semester: *Language Models for Quantum Simulation*, with visiting speaker [Roger Melko](https://rgmelko.github.io/) (University of Waterloo). Roger's talk will be at 2:00pm ET, in the MIT Kolker Room and on Zoom. Check out the talk abstract in the "Events This Week" section below.

Earlier in the day, IAIFI students and postdocs are invited to an informal, catered networking lunch with Roger. The lunch will be at 12:30pm at the IAIFI Penthouse.

### First IAIFI Lunch Discussion this Thursday
Our IAIFI Lunch Discussion organizers, Sean Benevedes (MIT) and Victoria Zhang (MIT), will host our first event this coming Thursday, February 12, at 1:00pm at the Penthouse and on Zoom! The discussion is titled: *How do LLMs impact work for students & postdocs?* Check out the full abstract in the "Events This Week" section below.

Lunch and refreshments will be provided, and feel free to invite people from outside of IAIFI you think may be interested!
