### IAIFI Member-Led Community-Building Activities: up to 25 USD per person reimbursable for pre-approved events

Have an idea for a community building event you'd like to organize? If the Community Forum approves the event (per the guidelines linked below), then IAIFI participants will receive reimbursement after the event, up to 25 USD per participant, maximum 250 USD per event. At least 5 IAIFI members must participate in the event to be eligible for reimbursement.

[Learn More / Submit an Activity](https://iaifi.org/social-activities){:.button.button--outline-primary.button--pill.button--sm}

### This Friday: Thematic Discussion Session on Geometric Deep Learning

This event will begin with three lightning talks (10 minutes each, plus short Q&A) regarding different aspects of Geometric Deep Learning, followed by a guided discussion. Speakers will include: Akshunna Dogra (IAIFI Fellow), “Man, Machine, and Mathematics”; Melanie Weber (Harvard, IAIFI Affiliate), talk details to come; and Jie Xu (Northeastern), “The Geometry of Unlabeled Networks”. Check out the [details for each lightning talk.](https://tinyurl.com/49dc3ex4)

This event will be Friday, March 20, 2026, 2:00pm – 3:00pm in the MIT Kolker Room, 26-414.
