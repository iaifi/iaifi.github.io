## News from IAIFI Management

### Industry Lunch Talk on Friday, December 12 with Matthew Rispoli (PDT Partners)
Join us this coming Friday, December 12 at 12:30pm at the IAIFI Penthouse or on Zoom for an Industry Lunch Talk with [Matthew Rispoli (PDT Partners)](https://www.linkedin.com/in/mnrispoli/). During the lunch, Matthew will discuss his career trajectory and experience as a researcher at PDT Partners. Matthew completed his PhD in Physics at Harvard, and was a part of the Harvard-MIT Center for Ultracold Atoms. His research was focused on using quantum gas microscopy to study entanglement and thermalization within isolated quantum many-body systems. *Please note the speaker for this talk will be virtual, but lunch will be provided for all in-person attendees.*
