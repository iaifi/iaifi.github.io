### Forum Survey: IAIFI Research, Talent, and Community
Fill out the Forum Survey below to send in ideas, volunteer for opportunities, and provide input as we plan events for 2026. This includes the opportunity to **recommend invited speakers for the 2026 Summer Workshop!** Fill out the survey by **Friday, December 19** to be entered in a raffle for IAIFI merch.

### IAIFI Summer School 2026: Applications Open
Applications for the [2026 IAIFI Summer School](https://iaifi.org/phd-summer-school) are now open! **The Summer School will be held August 3–7, 2026 in Boston/Cambridge.** All IAIFI Junior Investigators are guaranteed acceptance at least once. Please apply if you are interested in attending and share with your networks! 

Also save the date for the **[2026 IAIFI Summer Workshop](https://iaifi.org/summer-workshop), which will be held August 10–14, 2026.** Registration will open in January 2026.
