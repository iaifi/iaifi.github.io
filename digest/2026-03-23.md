### April 3 Fellows Showcase and Social (RSVP by this Thursday, March 26)
Next Friday, April 3, 2026, we are holding two exciting events that are open to the IAIFI community! We hope all IAIFI members, and especially Senior and Junior Investigators,  will take this opportunity to hear about the work being done by the IAIFI Fellows, including possible opportunities to collaborate with them, and to network with other IAIFI members.

* IAIFI Fellows Showcase: 2:00-3:30pm, MIT Kolker Room (26-414) and Zoom
    * Featured Fellows: Akshunna Dogra, Berthy Feng, and Mathis Gerdes. [See the lightning talk abstracts here.](https://tinyurl.com/yprvk5w4).
* IAIFI Spring Social ([**Please RSVP by noon on March 26**](https://app.smartsheet.com/b/form/019cb48973277b9e841924fe85f3cc79)): 4:00-6:00pm at Mex Taqueria & Bar (500 Technology Square, Cambridge).
